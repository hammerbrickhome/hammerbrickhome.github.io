Painting FAQs

Do I supply the paint?

For Premium/Signature jobs, we recommend you purchase the paint so you control the exact color/sheen, or we can pick it up for you. We supply all plastic, tape, and spackle.

How long does a room take?

A standard 12x12 room takes 1 day for prep and paint. If skim coating is needed, it takes 2-3 days for drying time.

What is skim coating?

Skim coating is applying a thin layer of compound over the entire wall to hide texture, old roller marks, and damage. It makes the wall look like new glass.
