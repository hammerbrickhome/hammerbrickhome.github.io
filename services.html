Kitchen Remodel FAQs

How long does a typical luxury kitchen remodel take in NYC?

A full gut renovation for a kitchen typically takes **6-10 weeks** from demolition to final walk-through, depending on co-op/condo board approval timelines and the complexity of custom cabinetry and imported materials. We provide a guaranteed timeline in your written Signature or Premium proposal.

Are you certified to work on older, pre-1978 homes that may have lead paint?

Yes. Hammer Brick & Home LLC is an **EPA RRP Lead-Safe Certified Firm**. Our process includes strict containment, dust control, and cleaning verification to comply with all regulations for working in older NYC properties, which is crucial for kitchen work.

Can you handle the Co-op/Condo board approval process?

Absolutely. For our **Signature Tier** clients, we handle the entire alteration agreement process, including preparing architectural plans, liaising with the board’s managing agent, and submitting required insurance and license documentation.

What is the price range for a kitchen remodel?

The price range depends heavily on the chosen tier (Foundation, Premium, or Signature) and material choices. Generally, a full NYC kitchen remodel starts in the **$18,000 – $25,000** range for our Foundation Tier and goes up to **$45,000+** for a Signature, custom-designed space.
