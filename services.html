Certified Lead-Safe Compliance for NYC

In New York City, working on any structure built before 1978 requires strict adherence to EPA Renovation, Repair, and Painting (RRP) standards. We specialize in comprehensive containment, using air scrubbers and negative air pressure to eliminate the risk of lead dust. This is non-negotiable for safety and compliance in the **5 Boroughs**. We provide full documentation for your records.
