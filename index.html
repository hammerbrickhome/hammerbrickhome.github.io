Project Questions

Are you licensed and insured for work in NYC?

Yes. Hammer Brick & Home LLC is a fully licensed Home Improvement Contractor (HIC #2131291) with full General Liability and Workers' Comp insurance for Staten Island, Brooklyn, and all NYC boroughs.

How do your tiered estimates work?

We provide Foundation (essential), Premium (high-value), and Signature (luxury) options for every project. This lets you see the difference in material quality and finishing before you commit.

What is your cleanup process?

We use a 'White-Glove' approach. This includes floor masking with Ram Board or plastic, HEPA dust control during masonry, and a complete site sweep and wipe-down every single day.
