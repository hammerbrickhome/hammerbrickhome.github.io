How We Finalize Your Price

  • All pricing depends on access, parking, and building rules.
  • Material choices (standard vs. premium) can move numbers up or down.
  • Existing conditions, repairs, and hidden damage affect final cost.
  • Permits, DOB requirements, and inspections (when needed) are reviewed up front.

Every project receives a written, line-itemed proposal for your approval before work begins.
