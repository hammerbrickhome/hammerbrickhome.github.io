  • 14. Mailbox & House Number Upgrade – Remove old mailbox and numbers, patch old holes if needed, and install homeowner-provided modern replacements for a clean, updated entry look.
  • 15. Spring Patio Prep – Light wash of patio/stoop, sweeping debris, re-sanding paver joints, rearranging your existing furniture, and preparing your outdoor space for the season.
  • 16. Winter Slip-Resistant Step Package – Install anti-slip strips or grit tape on up to 4 stairs, check railing stability, and advise on snow/ice safety for winter months.
  • 17. Storm-Ready Drain & Water Check – Clear outdoor drains (2–3), inspect pooling and slope issues, test water flow, and provide drainage and waterproofing recommendations.
  • 18. Kid-Safe Home Check – A child-level assessment identifying dangers like sharp corners, loose furniture, unstable items, and exposed outlets. We install homeowner-provided safety covers.
  • 19. Rental / Airbnb 10-Point Condition Check – Quick inspection covering cleanliness, walls, doors, steps, railings, bathrooms, safety hazards, and overall guest readiness — plus a mini condition report.
  • 20. Outdoor Oasis Concept Sketch – A layout concept for your backyard or patio including seating zones, grill area, lighting ideas, walkway flow, and privacy suggestions.
  • 21. Luxury Entry Mood Board – Two design themes (Modern & Classic) with curated color schemes, material samples, lighting ideas, and inspiration photos for upgrading your entrance.
  • 22. “Photo-Ready” Living Room/Entry Touch-Up – Patch small holes, touch up minor paint (with your paint), clean scuffs, straighten decor, and prepare the space for photos or guests.
  • 23. Pre-Sale Curb Appeal Audit – Identify top 5–7 inexpensive exterior improvements (paint touch-ups, rail cleaning, mailbox upgrades, walkway cleaning) to help your property show better when selling.
  • 24. No-Trip-Fee Emergency Check Pass – One emergency on-site visit with no trip charge or diagnostic fee. We evaluate the issue and explain your options clearly.
  • 25. VIP Priority Booking – Access to faster scheduling, priority response, and next-available booking preference for future paid work, all year long.

Rules & Exclusions

Must be owner-occupied. No rentals, vacant properties, or businesses. One perk per household per month.

All perks are small, quick, non-technical tasks that can be safely completed in under one hour.

No ladders over 6 ft, no live electric, gas, chemicals, pests, or roof access.

If a perk is unsafe, too large, or requires skilled labor, it may be declined and a paid estimate offered.

Outdoor perks may be postponed due to weather. Monthly availability is limited.

Homeowner must provide safe access, move pets, unlock gates, and remove hazards.

Labor is included. Materials must be supplied by the homeowner unless stated otherwise.

We are not responsible for old or fragile items that break under normal work conditions.

Basic before/after photos may be taken unless you request opt-out before the visit.

Perks cannot be stacked, sold, transferred, or repeatedly used on the same recurring issue.

Two winners each month. Must live in our service area. No cash value. Non-transferable.

Hammer Brick & Home LLC may update, pause, or end perks at any time.
