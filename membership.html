Rules & Exclusions

One perk per household per month. Must be owner-occupied. No rentals, businesses, or vacant properties. Proof of address may be required.

All perks are small, quick, non-technical tasks that can be performed safely in under one hour.

No ladders over 6 ft, live electrical, gas, pests, chemicals, roof access, or hazardous work.

If a perk is unsafe or larger than allowed, it may be declined and a paid estimate offered.

Outdoor perks may be postponed due to weather. Monthly availability is limited.

Homeowners must provide safe access, move pets, unlock gates, and clear hazards.

Labor is included. Homeowner supplies any required materials unless noted.

We are not responsible for old, fragile, or damaged items that worsen during normal work.

Basic before/after photos may be taken for records unless you opt out in advance.

Perks may not be combined, sold, saved for later, or used repeatedly on the same issue.

Winners must live in our service area. No cash value. Cannot be transferred.

Hammer Brick & Home LLC may update or discontinue perks at any time.
