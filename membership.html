Rules & Exclusions

Our technician is scheduled for a strict 1-hour window. We will do as much quality work as possible in 55 minutes. If a task requires more time due to unforeseen conditions, we will stop and provide a quote to finish, or leave it in a safe, stable condition.

We provide the labor, tools, and basic consumables (screws, glue, cleaning spray). You must provide specific hardware (light fixtures, new locks, paint, batteries, etc.) ready at the start of the appointment.

For insurance reasons, we cannot perform work on roofs, or on ladders higher than 8 feet. We do not touch live electrical wiring behind walls or active gas plumbing.

Must be owner-occupied. One perk per household per month. Rentals/Tenants must have landlord approval.

Unsafe or oversized perks may be declined with a paid estimate option. We reserve the right to refuse service if conditions are hazardous.

Outdoor perks may be postponed due to weather. Scheduling is based on crew availability in your zip code.

We are not responsible for fragile, rotted, or failing existing conditions that break during standard repair attempts.

Perks cannot be stacked, repeated within 6 months, transferred, or exchanged for cash value.

Hammer Brick & Home LLC may update, pause, or end the perks program at any time without prior notice.
