Rules & Exclusions

  • Eligibility: One perk per household per month within our approved service radius. Proof of address may be required. Perks are intended for owner-occupied residential homes only.
  • Light-Duty Only: All perks are small, basic, non-technical tasks designed to be completed in under one hour. No full projects, repairs, replacements, or labor requiring permits.
  • Safety Limitations: No hazardous work, no roofs, no ladders over 6 ft, no gas lines, no live electrical, no structural work, no heavy lifting, and no work involving mold, pests, or chemicals.
  • Condition & Scope Check: If a task is unsafe, inaccessible, or larger than the perk allows, it will be paused. A written paid estimate may be offered instead. We may decline any perk that cannot be performed safely.
  • Weather & Scheduling: Outdoor perks depend on weather and daylight. Appointment availability is limited and not guaranteed each month. Some perks may require rescheduling.
  • Access Requirements: Clear access is required to complete the perk. Homeowners are responsible for moving vehicles, unlocking gates, removing hazards, and ensuring safe pathways. Any parking/dumpster fees are the homeowner's responsibility.
  • Tools & Materials: Perks include basic labor only. If materials are needed (paint, bulbs, cleaners, etc.), the homeowner must provide them unless noted otherwise. No tool drop-offs or material pickups.
  • No Liability for Pre-Existing Issues: We do not accept responsibility for hidden damages, aging materials, loose fixtures, fragile items, or pre-existing conditions that may worsen during normal light-duty work.
  • Photos & Documentation: Before/after photos may be taken for records and quality verification. You may opt out by written request before the visit begins.
  • Fair Use: Membership perks may not be combined, transferred, banked, resold, or used for rental properties, businesses, or repeated tasks beyond intended monthly limits.
  • Giveaways & Drawings: Two (2) member drawings or giveaways may be offered each month. Winners must reside within our service radius. Prizes have no cash value and may not be transferred.
  • Right to Modify or End Program: Hammer Brick & Home LLC reserves the right to update, change, limit, or discontinue any perk or the entire membership program at any time without prior notice.
  • Final Interpretation: All perks are provided at our discretion. The latest version of these rules on this page controls and overrides any previous versions.
