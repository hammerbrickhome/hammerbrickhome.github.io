Payment FAQs

Do you offer financing through banks like Hearth?

No. We are an independent, family-owned business. We do not partner with third-party lenders that charge our neighbors high interest rates. We strictly offer 0% interest progress payments.

Can I pay for materials directly?

Yes! For "Signature" tier projects, many clients prefer to buy their own tiles, vanities, or fixtures directly to control the budget. We simply charge for the labor to install them.

What happens if the project is delayed?

You don't pay. Our payment schedule is based on work completed, not dates on a calendar. If we haven't reached the milestone, you don't write the check. This protects you completely.
