Bathroom FAQs

What comes first, the floor or the vanity?

We typically install the finished floor first so it runs under the vanity. This protects the subfloor and looks cleaner.

Can you move my toilet?

Yes, but moving a toilet stack requires a DOB permit and significant plumbing work. Keeping the layout saves money.

Do you install steam showers?

Yes, for Signature projects. Steam showers require ceiling waterproofing, a vapor barrier, and a specialized generator.
