Historic Preservation & Restoration

Owning a home in Park Slope, Bed-Stuy, or Carroll Gardens requires a contractor who understands old-world materials. We don't just patch; we restore.

  • Stoop Resurfacing: We repair crumbling brownstone steps using correct composite mixes to match the original color.
  • Brick Pointing: We grind out old mortar and replace it to stop leaks and restore structural integrity.
  • Bluestone & Pavers: Authentic bluestone installation for sidewalks and rear gardens.
  • Garden Apartments: Waterproofing and under-stoop storage renovation.
