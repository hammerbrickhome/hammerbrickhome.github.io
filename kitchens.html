Kitchen FAQs

Do I need a DOB permit for my kitchen?

Only if you are moving plumbing/gas lines or removing walls. Direct replacements usually do not require permits.

How long does a kitchen remodel take?

A facelift takes 3-5 days. A full gut renovation typically takes 3-5 weeks depending on materials.

Can you install IKEA cabinets?

Yes! We are experienced with IKEA systems, as well as semi-custom and fully custom millwork.
